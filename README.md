# neobank-notes

Personal research notes comparing neobanks and digital banking apps.
Updated as I test them. Not financial advice.

## Apps covered

| App | Type | FDIC | Monthly fee |
|-----|------|------|-------------|
| Chime | Neobank | Yes (via Bancorp/Stride) | $0 |
| Cash App | Payment + investing | Yes (via Sutton Bank) | $0 |
| Venmo | P2P payments | No | $0 |
| SoFi | Full digital bank | Yes | $0 |

## Key things I learned

- FDIC insurance is on the partner bank, not the neobank itself
- - Fee-free overdraft is a real differentiator
  - - APY rates change often, verify before opening an account
